Chapter 4, all natural, organic self-confidence
Mindfullness and self awareness are essentially the same thing, minfullness is an activity that results in awareness. Both can be trained in similar ways which is what was explored in this chapter. I tried the Body Scan practice, during the practice I felt very relaxed and by the end felt happy. I don't often just sit and check in with how I'm feeling and I think it's really important to take the time to sit and check in with yourself. It makes a huge difference to your day. What I learnt from the last section of the chapter is that emotions are physiologoical experiences that we can control. We can calm ourselves down, or ignore them but they don't define who we are.

Problem Solving Techniques
I got blocked on one of the problems in the theory for sprint 5 - Fizz Buzz. I used pseudocode to write down what I wanted the programme to do then went back into my notes that I had written from the basic javascript course on freecodecamp to figure out what would work best. I knew that I needed a loop, and an if/else statement and from there I started to fill in the gaps. I felt good about the process because I had all the information I needed to solve the problem, it was just a matter of applying the right solution. It was really satisfying when it worked. I learnt that the remainder operator (%) is a way to test whether a number is divisible by another number.
